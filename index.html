About Us

Zdravo and welcome to the beautiful Kvarner Bay! Water Sport Centar Selce is your expert for fun water sport activities and makes your time in Selce to an amazing experience! located Here, in a cosy village of Selce, near the Maslina beach, the turquoise waters of the Adriatic Sea create together with the picturesque landscape a perfect spot for all holiday-makers who love sea and fun. With children or friends, it doesn't matter, you can spend 10 minutes or as much you wish on our inflatable boats: yellow banana boat, fast tubes or crazy ufo. Or, are you more a ski lover? Why not trying skiing on the water then? Trust us, it's a completely different experience! Maybe you dream about flying above people's heads on a parachute? No problem! Our motto is: a holiday without fun is not a holiday!
