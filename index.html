Welcome aboard

Watersports the way they should be — flexible, friendly & full of thrill

At Water Sport Centar Selce we live for the sea. Whether you are chasing an adrenaline rush or a gentle glide with the family, our team makes every ride safe, simple and unforgettable. Just walk down to Plaža Maslina, pick your adventure and we take care of the rest.

  • Experienced, safety-first crew & modern equipment
  • Rides for every age — from calm cruises to wild splashes
  • Right on the beachfront promenade in Selce

Pick Your Adventure

Four ways to play on the water — choose the thrill that fits your mood and we will get you riding the Adriatic in no time.

Sky-high

Parasailing

Combine the thrill of flying with the serenity of floating over water. Towed gently behind our boat beneath a colourful canopy, you will drift high above the bay for views you will never forget.

Book this ride →
Adrenaline

UFO

An out-of-this-world ride that takes tubing to a whole new level. Hold on tight as you skim and bounce across the waves for an electrifying rush of pure speed and laughter.

Book this ride →
Easy fun

Tube

Climb aboard the inflatable ring and let our boat tow you across the sea. It is simple, it is fun and it is all about enjoying the ride — perfect for first-timers and families.

Up to 9

Banana Slider

Pile up to nine friends onto our banana-shaped inflatable and hang on for the twists, turns and refreshing splashes. Built tough for big groups and bigger laughs.
